Getting to the Ceremony & Reception

We have reserved 6 shuttles dedicated to picking up and dropping off our guests safely the day/night of the wedding. It is approximately 20 minutes drive time from Edwards and 30 minutes from Avon.

The shuttles will be picking up guests in Avon and Edwards at 3:30pm. The shuttles will return guests to Avon and Edwards at staggered times throughout the evening (Exact times TBD).

All guests are encouraged to use the shuttle service. There is limited parking at the venue and we would like our guests to return home safely after a couple celebratory drinks!

Where to Stay

Inn and Suites at Riverwalk

27 Main Street, Edwards, CO 81632

The Inn and Suites at Riverwalk is the closet hotel to the venue and the only hotel in the town of Avon. It was renovated this year and is located right on the river!

We have reserved a room block and a group rate. You may book through the group rate or look at other sites where you may find cheaper prices (Priceline, hotels.com, etc).

A shuttle will be departing from this location the afternoon of the wedding and returning guests here following the reception.

Sheraton Mountain Vista Villas - Avon

160 W Beaver Creek Blvd, Avon, CO 81620

The Sheraton is a beautiful hotel in Avon. It is located in the center of town near a park and a lake. It is a more expensive option, but cheaper rates are likely to be found on other websites such as Priceline, Hotels.com, etc.

There is not a room block at this hotel; however, a shuttle will be available to pick up guests the day of the wedding.

AirBnb

There are plenty of options for rental condos and homes in the towns of Edwards and Avon. Shuttles will be available for transport to and from the Venue near the center of town in both Avon and Edwards. Consider booking an AirBnB and walking to the shuttle pick up!

Comfort Inn Near Vail Beaver Creek

161 W Beaver Creek Blvd, Avon, CO 81620

The Comfort Inn is an economical option in Avon. It is located in the center of town near a park and a lake - directly across the street from the Sheraton. This hotel is not the nicest option in town; however, you can typically find great deals online! This is our go-to in Avon and we can typically get a room for ~$100.00/night on Priceline. Plus, there are free waffles in the mornings!

There is not a room block at this hotel; however, a shuttle will be available to pick up guests the day of the wedding.
