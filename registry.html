The very best present we can receive is your presence at our wedding and your love and support as we embark on this adventure together. If you feel inclined to give a gift, we ask that you consider donating to a cause that we are passionate about. We are very fortunate and would like to share our good fortunes amongst the world. We have set up a registry at The Good Beginnings with links to three causes close to our hearts.

The Coral Reef Alliance (CORAL)

The Coral Reef Alliance (CORAL) united global communities to save coral reefs. It was founded in 1994 by a group of scuba divers committed to promoting sustainable tourism in coral reef destinations. CORAL is spearheading conservation strategies that address the long-term survival of coral reefs through today's changing environment. We have seen first hand the devastation to our Ocean's reefs and we are passionate about finding creative solutions that will protect our reefs for future generations.

Denver Dumb Friends League

Denver Dumb Friends League is an organization within our own community working to end pet homelessness and animal suffering. DDFL is a national leader in pet adoption, maintains a no-suffering and pre-emptive care organization and is a preventive organization dedicated to reducing the number of homeless pets through various programs and educational resources. Our Miss Mila is one spoiled dog, but we recognize that not every dog gets a similar life. We are passionate about protecting the animals in our community.

Colorado Public Radio (CPR)

Colorado Public Radio (CPR) produces and curates in-depth and meaningful news and music, establishing thoughtful connections to Colorado for listeners seeking to be informed, enlightened and entertained. We have been donating to CPR for 6 years - even when we could barely afford it! We are passionate about sharing stories amongst the community and thoughtful, ethical journalism.
